About R.E. Martin Tree Specialists, Inc.
R.E. Martin Tree Specialists, Inc. has been serving the Northern Virginia area since 1987. Founded by Russell E. Martin ('Rusty'), we are a family-owned and operated company with 38 years of experience in providing exceptional tree care services.
Our Services
Tree Removal: Safe and efficient removal of trees of all sizes.
Stump Grinding: Complete removal of tree stumps to restore your landscape.
Tree Trimming & Pruning: Expert pruning and trimming to enhance tree health and appearance.
Storm Cleanup: Prompt and efficient cleanup after storms.
Land Clearing & Excavation: Site preparation for construction and development.
Demolition: Professional demolition services for residential and commercial properties.
Snow Removal: Reliable snow removal services during winter months.
