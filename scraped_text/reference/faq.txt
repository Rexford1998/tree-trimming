Frequently Asked Questions
R.E. Martin Tree Specialists
Request Service
What services do you offer?
We offer a full suite of tree and site-prep services, including:
Tree removal, trimming, and pruning
Stump grinding and removal
Tree spading (relocating trees)
Forestry mulching and land clearing
Demolition services
Equipment rentals and commercial snow removal
Site preparation for residential & commercial projects
These services help with safety, aesthetics, and property enhancement.
Do you provide emergency tree services?
Yes. We offer 24/7 emergency tree services for urgent situations, such as fallen limbs, storm damage, or hazardous trees. Call us anytime to request fast, professional support.
Which areas do you serve?
We are based in Catharpin, VA, and serve surrounding areas including Chantilly, Centreville, Gainesville, and other parts of Northern Virginia.
How long have you been in business?
We have over 25 years of experience as a family-operated tree service company committed to safe, high-quality work and customer satisfaction.
Why should I hire a professional tree company instead of DIY?
Tree work can be dangerous. A professional team ensures:
Safety for you, your property, and your neighbors
Proper equipment and techniques
Permitting and environmental compliance
Better outcomes in tree health, aesthetics, and property value
How do I request a quote or schedule your services?
You can reach us by phone at (703) 830-5500 to discuss your project. Or visit our Contact page to submit your details, our team will follow up promptly.
